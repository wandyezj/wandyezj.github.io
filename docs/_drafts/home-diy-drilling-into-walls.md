# Home DIY - Drilling Into Walls

Need to be careful there are various services behind walls that you don't want to hit

- Electric
- Water
- Exhaust Vents
- Gas
- Sewage
- Network
- Speaker Wire
- HVAC
- Minisplit pipes


- Pipes can be made of different materials:
    - PVC
    - Copper
    - Iron
    - Aluminum

Cannot assume what is behind the wall. You can cause significant damage to your home if you drill into something accidentally.

There may be building codes about where things should be located, but they aren't always followed.

Know where your Electric, Water, and Gas shutoffs are.

Wall Materials:

- Drywall
- Plywood
- Lathe & Plaster


Detectors:

- Stud Detector
- Voltage Detector
- Metal Detector
